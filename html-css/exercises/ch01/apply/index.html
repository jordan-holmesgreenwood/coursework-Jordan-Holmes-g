To plan for a website you must first find the purpose of your creation and who is it going to target. Following this willcome your mission statement which will most likely explain who your target and purpose is. Now you must decide if you want multiplatform display which is HIGHLY reccomended for optimal viewing with more than 80% of americans owning a mobile device. Next is how you want your webpage main elements to look which is the wireframe. Then you want to make sure the structure of your page or sitemap is identified with the information pertaining to yoour website. Add graphics to improve to looks and reel the visitor in. Make sure you have clear navigation around the website with color and easy accessibility and addressing any issues as they come.

In this chapter i learned how to start creating and planning a webiste and what it really consists of from text editors to jQuery.
